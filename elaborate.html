ELABORATE

Students will work independently . They will apply their Knowledge  and understanding to find special cases of parallelogram.

The teacher will ask the following questions. 

If all the angles inside a parallelogram are 90° what shape it is? 

If all the sides and angles of a parallelogram are equal then what shape it is ?

If all the sides of  a parallelogram are equal then what shape it is?
