EXPLAIN

The students will explain what they exploredin their activities and discuss the below questions.

SIDES 

Students will discuss and explain the following questions.

Whether all the sides are equal ?

Whether the opposite sides are equal?

Whether the opposite sides are parallel?

DIAGONALS 

Students will discuss and explain the following questions.

Whether  the diagonals  are equal ?

Whether the diagonals bisect each other ?

ANGLES 

Students will discuss and explain the following questions.

Whether all the angles are equal ?

Whether the opposite angles are equal ?

What is the sum of their adjacent angles?
