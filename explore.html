MAKING A PARALLELOGRAM - 2 methods

Duration:
05:00
Grouping:
2

The teacher demonstrates how to make parallelogram using papers without any measurements.

MAKE A PARALLELOGRAM USING ANY OF THE FOLLOWING METHODS.



The teacher divides the students into three groups.                  

Group 1 : Sides

Group 2 : Diagonals

Group 3 : Angles

Each group has to explore their respective properties .

Task1 :    Before dragging any  points,just by enabling respective check boxes they 

                 should fill their respective tables.

Task 2:    After dragging some points they have to measure and fill the same details  

                 again.
